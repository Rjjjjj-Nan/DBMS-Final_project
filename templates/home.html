Locate. Link. Reclaim.

LostLink is a lost and found system designed to help users report, search, and recover lost or found items efficiently. It provides a centralized platform where people can record item details and track their status until they are returned to their rightful owners. The system promotes honesty, organization, and sustainability by reducing the number of unclaimed or replaced belongings. Through technology, LostLink aims to build a more connected and responsible community.
